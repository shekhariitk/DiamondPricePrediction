About Diamond Price Predictor

What is this tool?

The Diamond Price Predictor is a machine learning-based application that estimates the price of a diamond based on its specifications. Our model analyzes key diamond characteristics to provide accurate price predictions.

Important Note

This tool provides estimates based on historical data and should not replace professional appraisal. Actual prices may vary based on market conditions and other factors.

Diamond Characteristics We Analyze

The 4Cs of Diamonds
  • Carat Weight: The size/weight of the diamond
  • Cut: Quality of the diamond's proportions and symmetry
  • Color: Degree of colorlessness (D being most colorless)
  • Clarity: Presence of inclusions and blemishes
Physical Dimensions
  • Depth %: Height from culet to table
  • Table %: Width of the table facet
  • Length (x): Measurement in mm
  • Width (y): Measurement in mm
  • Depth (z): Measurement in mm

How It Works

Our prediction model uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of diamond transactions to identify price patterns. The model considers all the key factors that influence diamond pricing:

Model R2_Score

99% R2_Score

Our model achieves 99% R2_Score in predicting diamond prices based on testing with industry datasets. with mean absolute error of $0.37. with mean squared error of $80.96. This means it correctly estimates diamond prices within an acceptable range in 99 out of 100 cases.
